About Makar Sankranti

Makar Sankranti marks the transition of the sun into the zodiac sign of Capricorn. It is celebrated with kite flying, feasts, and joyous gatherings.

About Pongal

Pongal is a harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu. It signifies gratitude to nature and is marked by cooking the traditional Pongal dish.
